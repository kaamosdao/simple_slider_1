The unnamable is the eternally real. Naming is the origin of all particular things.
All phenomena bear the mark of Emptiness. Their true nature is the nature of no Birth no Death, no Being no Non-being, no Defilement no Purity, no Increasing no Decreasing.
Happiness is your nature. It is not wrong to desire it. What is wrong is seeking it outside when it is inside.
Hatred is, indeed, never appeased by hatred in this world. It is appeased only by loving-kindness. This is an ancient law.
In the dark night of all beings awakes to Light the tranquil man. But what is day to other beings is night for the sage who sees.
